Random Number Generator

This tool generates random numbers and assigns participants into groups for research studies by assigning a number between 0 and 1. If assigned a number from 0–0.6, the participant is placed in the treatment group. If assigned a number greater than 0.6–1, the participant is placed in one of the three control groups.

Participants are divided into a treatment group (60%) and three control subgroups (13.33% each). After generating group assignments, you can enter a list of participant IDs to match them with their group assignments and download the results as a CSV file.
